Here is a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from work due to health reasons. Unfortunately, I am currently not feeling well and am unable to come to work.

I am suffering from [mention the illness/disease, e.g. fever, flu, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take [number] days of rest to recover from my illness. I am expecting to be back to work on [date].

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I return. I will provide a medical certificate from my doctor as required.

Please let me know if there are any issues or concerns. I appreciate your understanding and support in this matter.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that this is a sample and you should customize it according to your specific situation and needs. Also, be sure to keep a polite and professional tone throughout the letter.
